Where do Austrians move to?

Every year, hundreds of thousands of people change their place of residence within Austria — moving from urban areas to rural regions, from west to east, or to one of the country’s major cities. This interactive map visualizes these migration movements at the federal state and district levels, for individual years or across the entire observation period. While some regions experience strong population growth, others are affected by population decline due to out-migration.

Select a year to explore how migration patterns have changed over time. The data displays net-migration — the difference between in-migration and out-migration. blue areas indicate population gains, while red areas indicate population losses.

01 — Overview

Where Do People Move?

Every year, more than 200.000 Menschen in Austria change their place of residence. The map shows net migration by federal state - blue areas gain population, while red areas experience population loss.

02 — Districts

Austria's 94 Districts

At the district level, the pattern becomes even more apparent: metropolitan areas around Vienna, Graz, and Linz continue to grow, while more peripheral districts often experience population loss due to out-migration.

03 — Comparison

In-Migration and Out-Migration by Federal State

Each district is represented by a point. Points to the right of the center experience more in-migration than out-migration, while points to the left are losing population.

04 — Absolute Numbers

How Many People Actually Move?

Behind every net migration value are real migration movements: out-migration to the left, in-migration to the right. Federal states with both high in-migration and high out-migration are particularly dynamic — people are moving in and out in large numbers.

05 — Net Migration Ranking

Who Gains, Who Loses?

In-migration minus out-migration results in net migration — the overall balance of each federal state. Positive values indicate population growth, while negative values indicate population decline. Federal states are ranked according to this value.

What about your Municipality?

The map shows the big picture — but behind every federal state are hundreds of municipalities, each with its own story. Is your municipality growing, or is it losing population? In the Municipality Explorer, you can explore each of Austria’s more than 2,000 municipalities in detail.
